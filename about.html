Meet Our Owner

Hello, my name is Anshu Chatterjee and I am the inventor of LPUEve, a Website inspired by my own personal event booking and registering experience. Spending the time in promoting for the events from classes to classes and asking people to register were bad but times when i missed an event that i was interested in because no proper platfrom to know about the happening events were worst. Often times I would return tired with bad mood and I knew it was time for a change! So I thought why there is a need to go to classes to promote or why miss your event just because you dont know its happening in this modern era of technology. And hence, we are here today, Register at first chance for your favourite events or register your own event sitting on your couch without any worry just in couple of minutes.
